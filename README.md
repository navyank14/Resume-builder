## üöÄ Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- No server setup required - runs entirely in the browser

### Installation
1. Download or clone the project files
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. Start building your resume!

### Usage

#### 1. Choose Your Template
- Browse through available templates
- Each template has unique features and styling
- Select the one that matches your industry and style

#### 2. Fill Out the Form
- **Personal Information**: Enter your contact details and social profiles
- **Professional Summary**: Write a compelling 2-3 sentence summary
- **Education**: Add your academic background
- **Experience**: Detail your work history
- **Skills**: List your technical and soft skills
- **Projects**: Showcase your portfolio projects
- **Certifications**: Add your professional achievements

#### 3. Upload Files
- **Profile Photo**: Upload a professional headshot
- **Certificates**: Upload relevant certifications
- **Portfolio Files**: Add writing samples or other documents

#### 4. Preview and Export
- Review your resume in real-time
- Make adjustments as needed
- Print or save your completed resume

## üìã Best Practices

### Resume Writing Tips
- Keep your summary concise (2-3 sentences)
- Use action verbs for experience descriptions
- Quantify achievements when possible
- Tailor content to the job you're applying for
- Proofread carefully for errors

### File Upload Tips
- Use high-quality profile photos (400x400px recommended)
- Ensure certificates are clearly readable
- Keep file sizes reasonable for faster loading
- Use descriptive filenames

### Template Selection
- **Professional**: Choose for corporate, government, or traditional roles
- **Modern**: Select for tech, creative, or startup positions
- **Custom**: Use when you need maximum flexibility
